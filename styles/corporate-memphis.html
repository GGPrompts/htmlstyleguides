The Corporate Memphis Aesthetic

This style emerged from Facebook's "Alegria" design system and quickly spread across tech companies. It's characterized by flat illustrations of diverse people in playful poses, organic blob shapes, and a bright color palette. While once ubiquitous, it became so overused that it's now often parodied - making it a fascinating study in design trends.
