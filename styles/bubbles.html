09

Design Principles

The four guiding ideas behind every bubbly decision in this design system.

◯
Round & Soft
No sharp edges, everything flows. Pill shapes, circles, and generous border-radius create a world where corners do not exist.
☆
Playful & Joyful
Bouncy animations, bright colors, and whimsical language. Every interaction should make you smile and want to do it again.
✨
Iridescent & Magical
Shimmer effects, rainbow highlights, and glassmorphism that captures the magical quality of real soap bubbles in sunlight.
✋
Touch-Friendly
Big targets (44px minimum), satisfying feedback on every tap, and generous spacing. Designed for little fingers and big imaginations.
