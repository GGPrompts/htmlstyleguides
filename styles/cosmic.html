Philosophy

Design Principles

The guiding stars of cosmic design.

01

Embrace the Void

Deep, dark backgrounds create infinite depth. Let negative space breathe like the vacuum between stars.

02

Celestial Glow

Every interactive element radiates with subtle luminescence. Light emerges from darkness like stars in the night sky.

03

Ethereal Motion

Animations are slow and graceful, like celestial bodies drifting through space. Nothing is jarring in the cosmos.

04

Nebula Depth

Layered gradients and backdrop blur create atmosphere. Each element exists at its own depth in the cosmic canvas.

05

Timeless Beauty

Elegant serif typography evokes the eternal. The cosmos predates us and will outlast us all.

06

Infinite Wonder

Every interaction should inspire awe. The universe is vast and beautiful beyond comprehension.
