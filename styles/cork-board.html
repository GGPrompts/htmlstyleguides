String Connections

Gather requirements and user feedback. Pin all findings to the board.

Step 2: Ideate

Brainstorm solutions. Every sticky note is a potential breakthrough.

Step 3: Prototype

Build a rough version. Tape mockups to the board for team review.

Step 4: Ship It

Launch, celebrate, and pin the victory memo to the board.
