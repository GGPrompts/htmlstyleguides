Design principles

Terrazzo Atelier balances organic texture with modern geometry. Keep layouts calm, then punctuate with one memorable accent per screen.

Warmth Clarity Balance

Layered surfaces

Use soft shadows and offset strokes to create depth without heavy contrast.

Handmade rhythm

Pair structured grids with small, imperfect accents like chips, tapes, or tags.

Quiet typography

Let body copy breathe. Make headlines feel editorial, but keep supporting text restrained.

Color palette

Muted bases with a handful of saturated chips. Use clay or ocean for CTAs, reserve sun for highlights.
