Ma Design System - The beauty of empty space

間 - 静寂 - 侘寂

Typography Colors Buttons Cards Forms Tables Alerts

In Japanese aesthetics, ma represents the pause between notes, the space between objects, the silence that gives meaning to sound. This design system embraces emptiness as a form of expression.

Ma

間

Negative space. The pause. What is not there defines what is.

Wabi-Sabi

侘寂

Beauty in imperfection. The worn, the weathered, the asymmetric.

Kanso

簡素

Simplicity. Eliminate the unnecessary. Keep only what matters.

Body text uses Noto Sans JP with light weight and generous line height. The goal is readability without distraction, allowing words to breathe on the page like ink settling on washi paper.

The Western companion typeface, Cormorant Garamond, brings classical elegance that harmonizes with Japanese aesthetics.
